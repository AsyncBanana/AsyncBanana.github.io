The Code Of Conduct

  1. Be Nice


  2. No spamming


  3. Do not post large amounts of pictures or GIFs (more than 5 a day) and make sure that their purpose is cohesive and they aren't random


  4. Be constructive


  5. No talking after 8:00 PM or before 7:00 AM


  6. Respect your peers and don't tease, bully, or use derogatory terms


  7. No talking during class unless somebody can't get in to class or there is a emergency


  8. Ask an admin if you have any questions
